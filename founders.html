Dear Students,

It is with great pleasure that I welcome you to Maharaja Agrasen Mahavidyalaya, a place where knowledge meets opportunity, and ambition transforms into achievement.

At MAM, we are committed to fostering a dynamic and inclusive learning environment that empowers our students to grow academically, personally, and professionally. With a dedicated faculty, modern facilities, and a vibrant campus life, we strive to ensure that each student receives a holistic education that prepares them for the challenges of the future.

Our college offers a wide range of academic programs, extracurricular activities, sports activity through Shri Agrasen Cricket Academy & Shri Agrasen Indoor sports Center and support services designed to nurture your talents and help you reach your full potential. Whether you are beginning your higher education journey or transferring from another institution, we are here to support you every step of the way.

We invite you to become a part of our community, where innovation, integrity, and excellence are not just values – they are a way of life.

Welcome to Maharaja Agrasen Mahavidyalaya – your future begins here.

Established in 1998 as a Science college, Maharaja Agrasen Mahavidyalaya has grown into a leading institution in the Rohilkhand region, offering a vibrant and inclusive academic environment. With a student strength of over 2200 and a commitment to quality education, we focus on Outcome-Based Education that emphasizes student learning through modern teaching methodologies and practical exposure.

At our core lies the vision of nurturing talent, promoting critical thinking, and preparing students to meet the demands of a dynamic global landscape. Our state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and holistic approach to learning ensure that students not only gain knowledge but also the skills to succeed.

Join us in this journey of academic excellence and innovation.

Dr. Praveen K. Agarwal (Ex Dean, Commerce – MJP Rohilkhand University)
