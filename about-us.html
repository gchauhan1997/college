About Us

Maharaja Agrasen Shiksha Samiti (Regd.)

Maharaja Agrasen Shiksha Samiti (Regd.), Bareilly is a charitable trust of socially dedicated trustees. On 4th February, 1995 Maharaja Agrasen Shiksha Samiti laid down the foundation stone of Maharaja Agrasen Mahavidyalaya at 11th k.m. milestone on Bareilly- Lucknow National Highway (NH-24). Maharaja Agrasen Mahavidyalaya started imparting education in 1998 with students of B.Sc. classes. After the continuous efforts of the faculty, staff and able guidance of members of Maharaja Agrasen Shiksha Samiti, College has evolved as one of the premier centre of higher education in Bareilly city, embracing more than 2200 students getting education in science, Arts, Commerce, Education, Computers and Management streams etc in 21 Courses. MAM also got the Recognition from AICTE for BBA and BCA Courses. We are also trying to impart computer accounting education to every student. We also Started Shri Agrasen Cricket Academy and Idea Café, Innovation Centre & NATS under the State and Central Government Scheme.

Facilities at Mantaram Mahavidyalaya

Mantaram Central Library

Mahavidiyalaya’s Central called “Mantaram Central Library is a very rich, managed, planned and computerized learning center for Information Technology, Management and Sciences having a collection of best books. Apart from above fields, it has a diverse specification of journals, magazines for other classes too. Library holds leading Indian and International journals and magazines with round the year facility.

Our Lecture Halls

Spacious, airy class rooms having learning aids viz., OHP, White Board, TV, Educational CD's, LCD and Slide Projector with sufficient and proper seating arrangement.

Our Seminar Hall

Adequate seating plans and capacity, purposely for debates, seminars, conferences and lectures.

Auditorium

MAM is state of art auditorium with seating capacity of 800 plus. It is used for all types of formal assembly like Guest lecture, award ceremonies etc.

Shri Agrasen Indoor Sports Centre

Mahavidyalaya is also providing facility of indoor sports for students to overcome them from their tiring schedule.

Bus Facility

College has its own bus facility for students, plying to and from every corner of city.

Cafeteria

College has a well-equipped canteen inside the campus in a hygienic environment, serving coffee/tea/soft drinks, natural fruit juice and snacks.

Scholarship

Maharaja Agrasen Shiksha Samiti (Regd.) awards scholarships to meritorious students. Mantaram scholarship for needy &meritorious students of six different courses. Diamond Medal is also introduced to the student who secure 85% and above marks in any courses.
